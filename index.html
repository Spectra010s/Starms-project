Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our catering services

What types of events do you cater?

We cater to a wide range of events including weddings, corporate functions, birthday parties, and more. Whether it's an intimate gathering or a large celebration, we have the expertise to make your event a success.

How far in advance should I book your catering services?

We recommend booking at least a week in advance for most events. For large events or peak seasons (holidays, wedding season), we suggest booking 2-4 weeks ahead to ensure availability.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions and allergies?

Absolutely! We can accommodate various dietary needs including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and other specific dietary requirements. Please inform us of any allergies or restrictions when booking.

What's included in your catering packages?

Our packages typically include food preparation, serving staff, basic serving equipment, and cleanup. We can also provide additional services like decorations, equipment rental, hotel reservation, event venues, security personnel/bouncers and event coordination for an additional fee.

Do you provide serving staff for events?

Yes, we provide professional serving staff for all our catering events. Our team is trained to deliver exceptional service and ensure your guests have a wonderful dining experience.

Can you create custom menus for events?

Yes, we can create custom menus tailored to your event's theme and dietary requirements. Our team will work with you to design a menu that fits your vision and satisfies your guests.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve the Osun metropolitan area and surrounding regions. For events outside our standard service area, we may charge additional delivery fees. Contact us to discuss your specific location.

How many guests can you accommodate?

We can accommodate events of various sizes, from intimate gatherings of guests to large celebrations of up to 500 guests or more-depending on preferred specifications. For very large events, we may recommend additional planning and coordination to ensure everything runs smoothly.
